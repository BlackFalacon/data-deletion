How to Request Data Deletion

If you have used our Facebook-integrated app and wish to delete your data, you can request it by contacting us.

Step 1: Send an email to: support@yourdomain.com

Step 2: In your email, please include:

  • Your Facebook User ID
  • The name of the app you used
  • A brief message requesting data deletion

We will verify your request and delete your data within 7 working days.
